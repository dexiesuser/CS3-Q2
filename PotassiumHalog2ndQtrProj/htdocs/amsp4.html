AM's Beneath the Boardwalk to The Car: A Deep Dive

A (not very) Short Message from the Author

As one of the biggest bands of this decade, I admire this band wholeheartedly. This whole website proves on how much I love the band and their music. Since it's such a versatile band, I can listen to various tracks of them depending on my mood. From mellow jazz album openings like 'There'd Better Be A Mirrorball' to chaotic rock headbangers such as 'Pretty Visitors,' Arctic Monkeys has so much to offer. I'm quite sad that I would not be able to afford tickets for their upcoming Asia Tour here in the Philippines. As long as they contrinue to put out amazing music, I'm alright with being a forever #TeamBahay for this band.
